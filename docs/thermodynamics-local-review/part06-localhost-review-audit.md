## Scientific corrections
- Macroscopic centre-of-mass kinetic energy is not part of internal energy
- External gravitational potential energy is not part of U
- “Potential energy is zero for an ideal gas” refined to intermolecular potential energy being neglected
- U = 3nRT/2 restricted to monatomic ideal gas under classical assumptions
- Isothermal ΔU = 0 stated only for ideal gas of fixed composition
- Absolute internal energy not claimed to be directly measurable
- Heat and work not treated as contents stored inside U
- Real-gas isothermal volume changes allowed to alter U
- Heat-capacity content restricted to the minimum relation needed for internal energy
