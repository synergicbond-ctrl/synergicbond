## Audit conclusion

The 13-part deployment was chemically useful but not a complete implementation of the new master specification. The largest missing blocks were atomic-orbital foundations (nodes, g/u, angular/radial functions), historical covalency models, detailed p–d/d–d/δ overlap, state-dependent inorganic structures, Bent/Drago depth, full multicentre families, detailed MOT species-by-species diagrams, pseudohalogens, clathrates, carbon allotropes/dangling bonds, and the complete silicate taxonomy. The rebuild therefore uses a new 23-part theory sequence plus Part 24 integrated practice rather than stretching the old 13-part order.
