## Scientific corrections
- Actual boundary work uses external pressure, not internal pressure in every process
- Pint may replace Pext only in the reversible limit
- Reversible motion requires an infinitesimal—not exactly zero—driving pressure difference
- ΔV = 0 eliminates only PV work, not all work modes
- Free expansion gives zero PV work despite finite internal pressure
- Chemistry and physics conventions are presented as alternative accounting systems
- Work is not stored as a state property
- Surface, electrical and elastic work are not forced to zero in a rigid vessel
- Sign and dimensional logic are explicitly checked
